Mynvestor's Fee Schedule

Stocks

Order Commission

Your Stock Broker charges a fee based on the value of shares bought or sold as follows:
2.0 % for trade value of less than K50,000
1.5 % for trade value of K50,001 to K100,000
1.0 % for trade value greater than K100,000

Regulatory Trading Fees

Capital Gains Tax

The profit you earn from the sell of your shares is called Capital gains.
There is 30 % capital gains tax on shares sold if the shares have been held for a period of less than 1 year. If the shares have been held for a period of more than 1 year, there is no tax applicable on the capital gain.

Value Added Tax

Value added tax is charged on top of the brokers commission.

Withholding Tax

There is 10% withholding tax deduction on dividends.

NOTE * Regulatory Trading Fees are set by law and regulation, and are subject to change without notice.
