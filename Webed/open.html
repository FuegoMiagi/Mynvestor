Open My Account

Let's open your Mynvestor account!

  1. Submit an application in your Mynvestor web app.
  2. In the coming days you’ll receive an email either confirming your application’s been approved, or asking for a bit more information.
  3. If we request a document to verify your identity, we’ll include instructions for uploading your documents securely. Please give us five to seven days to review the materials and open your account.
NOTE While submitting an application, you have the ability to queue one deposit before your account has been approved to ensure a smooth funding experience.

When this happens, we’ll wait until your account is officially approved before initiating the queued deposit from your bank account into your Mynvestor account.
Personal Information

Mynvestor requests personal information, including National ID, payslip and contact information, in order to comply with Malawi government laws and RBM rules. We’ll request personal information from both new customers and long-standing customers. Mynvestor complies with an MSE account opening rules. This rule requires Mynvestor to put procedures in place to verify the identity of any person seeking to open an account and to maintain records of their information.

We take the security of all data we collect very seriously. We don’t intend to use this data for anything other than the fulfillment of our regulatory requirements. Check out our data protection policies for more information.
