Verifying it's you


When you’re logging in or updating sensitive information, we may ask you for an additional verification code to make sure it's really you authorizing these updates.

Logging in to Mynvestor

When you’re logging in to your Mynvestor account, we’ll ask you to verify your login with a second piece of information in addition to your password. This will be a verification code sent to your phone number via text message. We do this to make sure that the login attempt is from a device that you have access to, and not from an attacker.

Updating sensitive information

When you update sensitive Mynvestor account information such as your email or phone number, we'll ask you to verify it's you through a verification code sent to your phone number via text message. We do this to make sure that you've authorized these updates.

Troubleshooting

What if I’m not getting my verification text messages?
  • Verify that your phone number is correct
  • Make sure your device has signal and an internet connection
  • Restart your phone and make sure no other apps are running in the background
  • Verify that your notifications are turned on for your device and make sure “Do not disturb” is turned off
  • If you are using a third-party phone security app, note that some block text messages and phone calls from unknown callers and might be preventing you from receiving verification codes—within any app you may be using, adjust your settings for text message filtering.
What if I don’t have access to the phone number that’s associated with my account?

You can change your phone number on Mynvestor directly from the app. You can also go to Contact Us > I have a different question > Account > Update your info > Phone Number to change it. When you change your phone number in order to log in, we'll ask you to verify your identity to ensure your account's security.

What if I don’t have access to the email that’s associated with my account?

You can change your email on Mynvestor as long as you have access to the phone number associated with your account. You can view the email address associated with your account in Settings, and update it if necessary
