What you need to get Started 15 Feb. 2015 11:03 PM
         

Individual Requirements

To apply for a Mynvestor account, you’ll need to meet the following requirements:

  • Be 18 years or older;
  • Have a valid National Identification Number or Drivers License Number;
  • Have a legal Malawian residential address within the 29 districts; and
  • Be a Malawian citizen or Malawian resident*.

Technology Requirements

Mynvestor is currently available only on web browsers.

Web

Mynvestor Web supports Chrome, Safari, Firefox, and Edge; and works on both Macs and PCs.
